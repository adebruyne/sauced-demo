The Restaurant

What if you didn’t have to argue over what to eat? Pizza? Tacos? Why not both? That's how we came up with the idea of Sauced.

Opened in May 2018 and based in Waynesville, North Carolina, 'Sauced' is a restaurant for people who love pizza, tacos and taps.

Conveniently located in the heart of downtown, 'Sauced' offers a menu to fulfill the needs of any cravings. We offer great food, rotating local drafts and a family friendly atmosphere. Stop by and try our popular “Tatchos”, before exploring the local shops in town. Or drop in for a refreshing beer after your hike in nearby Pisgah National Forest.

We promise, we won’t make you decide between tacos or pizza.
